Services and pricing

Inform Studio provides custom digital design services. Each project is quoted in writing before work begins. Prices depend on scientific complexity, number of deliverables, usage rights, schedule, and revision scope.

  • Scientific figures, diagrams, and technical schematics EUR 450-1,500
  • Explanatory illustrations for research, engineering, or public communication EUR 800-3,000
  • 2D or 3D scientific animation and motion graphics EUR 1,500-8,000
  • Journal covers, posters, presentation graphics, and visual identity support EUR 700-3,500
  • Visual communication consulting, review, and project planning EUR 120/hour

No payment is required through this website. Customers can submit a service request below and will receive a written estimate before any invoice or payment request is issued.

Terms, cancellations, and refunds

Terms and conditions
All work is custom digital design, illustration, animation, or consulting. Work begins only after a written quote has been accepted. Quotes define deliverables, fees, schedule, revision scope, payment terms, and usage rights.
Refund policy
Payments made before work begins are refundable. Deposits, milestone payments, and completed work are not refundable once the agreed work has begun. Incorrect charges or overpayments will be refunded where applicable.
Cancellation policy
Projects may be cancelled in writing by emailing sean@inform.studio. If a project is cancelled after work begins, completed work and committed project time remain payable.
Payment and delivery
Invoices are issued after quote acceptance or project milestones. Final digital files are delivered after approval and after any required final payment is received.
