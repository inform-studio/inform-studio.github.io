About

Hi! I'm Sean Kelley. I help scientists, researchers, and other folks in technical fields communicate their results clearly and effectively.

While studying to be a physicist, I realized that my favorite part of solving problems was drawing the diagrams! Now I’m fortunate to focus full-time on just that, producing technical images that aid scientists in exploring, documenting and communicating their research.

I'm a father and live in the Burgundy region of France with my family. Outside of work, I enjoy hiking, climbing, surfing (when I get the chance) and generally enjoying the outdoors. I'm also a multi-instrumentalist musician—currently learning the mandolin.

Services

With over a decade of experience providing scientifically accurate explanatory illustrations and animations for physics, engineering, chemistry, and other natural science fields, I can provide:

  • Technical diagrams and schematics
  • Explanatory illustrations for the general public
  • 2D/3D visualizations and animations
  • Scientific figures for journal articles
  • Journal covers
  • Poster design
  • Presentation design
  • Consulting on visual communication techniques and strategy
